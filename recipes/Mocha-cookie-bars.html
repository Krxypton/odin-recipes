Mocha Cookie Bars

About

These chocolaty cookie bars are somewhat of a cross between a brownie and a cookie, with a flavour reminisicent of Mexican hot chocolate. They're best if you err on the side of under-versus over-baking

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon instant espresso powder
  • 1 tablespoon strong brewed coffee
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoom sat
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter,sofetened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees celcius. Line an 8x8-inch square pan with enough parchment paper to have overhang on all sides
  2. Stir espresso powder and coffee together in a small bowl until espresso powder is completely dissolved
  3. Whisk flour,cocoa,cream of tartar,baking soda,salt, and 1/4teaspoon cinnamon together in a bowl;set aside.
  4. Beat butter 1/2 cup of white sugar, and brown sugar with electric mixer together until light and fluffy in a bowl. Beat in espresso mixture,egg, and vanilla until well combined. Stir in flor mixture;mix until a dough just begins to form. Press dough firmly and evenly into the prepared pan using lightly floured hands.
  5. Whisk 1 taablespoon white sugsr and remaining 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon together in a small bowl; sprinkle sugar mixture evenly over the dough
  6. Bake in the preheated oven just until the dough in the centre of the pan feels barely set, 20 to 30 minutes
  7. Allow cookies to cool in the pan for 10 minutes
